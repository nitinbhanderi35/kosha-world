            "content": "KĀLAKĀMUKHA¦ (KĀLAKĀMMUKA). A Rākṣasa. He was the brother of Prahasta, Rāvaṇa's Minister. (For Genealogy, see under Prahasta). During Śrī Rāma's life in the forest Kālakāmukha was also among Khara, Dūṣaṇa and others. The other eleven members of that group were—Śyenagāmī, Pṛthugrīva, Yajñaśatru, Vihaṅgama, Durjaya, Karavīrākṣa, Paruṣa, Meghamālī, Mahāmālī, Sarpāsya and Rudhirāśana.
