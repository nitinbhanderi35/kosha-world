So also <lang>Pali</lang> nivāseti, wrongly defined in <ls>PTSD</ls> <i>to dress oneself … to get clothed or dressed</i>. It is true that in the common cliché (kālyam eva, or the like) nivāsayitvā, <ls>LV</ls> 〔240.11〕 (<ab>vs</ab>); <ls>Mv</ls> 〔i.34.14〕; 〔54.10〕; 〔307.14〕; 〔iii.60.3〕; 〔255.15〕; 〔272.5〕; 〔414.5〕; or nivāsya, <ls>LV</ls> 〔407.13〕; <ls>Divy</ls> 〔20.2〕; <ls>Av</ls> 〔i.290.16〕, <ab>etc.</ab>; no object is expressed.
