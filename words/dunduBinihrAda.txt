            "content": "DUNDUBHINIHRĀDA¦. A powerful Rākṣasa, who was the son of Diti and the uncle of Prahlāda. When, in the fierce fight between the devas and the asuras the latter got absolutely defeated this Rākṣasa ascribed the reason for the failure to the brahmins and began killing them.
