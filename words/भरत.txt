his descendants as well as those who preceded him (?) were known as Bhāratas; the fame of Bhāratas was due to Bharata (<i>bharatād bhārātī</i> <i>kīrtir yenedaṃ bhārataṃ kulam / apare ye</i> <i>ca pūrve ca bhāratā iti viśrutāḥ</i>) 1. 69. 49; it was holy, brought wealth, fame, long life and heaven; it is also large and leads to bliss (<i>bhāratānāṃ mahaj janma śṛṇvatām</i> <i> … /nāsti vyādhibhayaṃ teṣāṃ paraloka-</i> <i>bhayaṃ kutaḥ // dhanyaṃ yaśasyam āyu-</i> <i>ṣyaṃ svargyaṃ puṇyaṃ tathaivaca</i>) 1. 56. 24-25;
